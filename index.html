About Us

2020年，长沙染色体娱乐集团有限公司正式成立，公司将以「上海」为总部，以 传统艺人经纪 和 练习生培训选拔 为两大主要经营板块，在未来十年间努力建设完成“行业内首个拥有自己完整产业链闭环的大型娱乐经纪集团”的经营目标

In 2020, Changsha Chromosome Entertainment Group Co., Ltd was formally established.The company will be headquartered in Shanghai.The two main business segments are traditional artist brokerage and trainee training and selection.In the next ten years, we will strive to complete the business goal of "the first large entertainment brokerage group with its own complete industrial chain closed loop in the industry"
